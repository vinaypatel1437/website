Eligibility & Application Process

  • Who is eligible to apply for AirCampus program?

    1. General -

    a. You are required to have a laptop and a stable internet connection.
    b. You should be available 8 pm - 11 pm, Mon to Friday, and will be able to for a period of 6 months to attend live classes for 2-3 hours and 2-3 hours of self-work for assignments and projects every day (5 days a week)

    2. Academic Background - You should be in the final or pre-final year of graduation OR Graduate looking for jobs OR working professional looking for better growth in the career.

  • Does AirCampus help me to get a job?

    a. We do everything we possibly can! We have 50+ hiring partners and a full team dedicated to the placement and career development. They help you prepare a resume and portfolio, practice interviews and even help you in salary negotiation. At the end of the day though, you're the one who needs to apply, interview and land the job.
    b. We provide you unlimited mock interviews
    c. Get 100% support from our dedicated placement team till you get placed
    d. Resume, Linkedin and portfolio building

  • What is the duration of the program?

    a. Duration: 24-32 Weeks
    b. Timing: Monday - Friday, 8pm- 11pm

  • How does the application process look like?

    Our highly selective admission process helps us bring together brilliant minds for this journey. Admissions are on a rolling basis - we accept you if we're convinced you're a great fit without waiting for the deadline. Which involves -

    1. Sign up - Sign up by filling the application form

    2. Cognitive Ability Test - Attempt 15 minutes Cognitive Ability Test. Aptitudes tested - Verbal, Numerical, and Spatial reasoning. This test will measure your Critical thinking, problem solving skills, learning ability and aptitude for applying new information. Read important details about test below -

    a. You have 50 questions to solve in 15 minutes; that's 18 seconds per question!
    b. There is no penalty for a wrong answer. So you are encouraged to manage your time and to choose random answers for questions that are more challenging and that require extra amount of time - you can spend the gained time on other easier questions.
    c. There is no penalty for a wrong answer. Once you have answered a question, you cannot go back to it later on.
    d. Ideally, to make it through to the next step of your application process, you should aim for a minimum of 30 correct answers.

    2. Personal interview - Those who clear the first round will move to the next step i.e Personal Interview via Zoom and the objective of the interview is to understand your motivation to learn.

    If you pass all the previous steps, you will be invited to become a student in our main batch for 24-32 weeks. We guarantee a job for all of our students from this stage.
